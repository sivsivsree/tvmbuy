ABOUT

Tvmbuy is an online shopping market in trivandrum city where you can choose from a wide range of products related to your home needs ( Grocery, Fruits & Vegetables, Cosmetics, household,Stationery,recharge coupons,foo items etc) and allows you to place your order online and get them delivered at your door step.

First and foremost, we do only FREE deliveries. Once you place the order, we will confirm the order with you through phone. Daily 1 PM we consolidate the orders till then and sort the orders for delivery. As of now we have two delivery slot between 10pm - 1pm & 5pm - 8pm. Our delivery vans are charted to route through different locations in city. Orders inclusive of vegetables/meat/fish before 1 PM will be delivered the same day. Other orders before 3 PM will be delivered the same day. Orders inclusive of meat should be placed before 10 PM the previous day. Price of meat and fish may vary according to the actual market price and the quantity delivered. Delivery of meat and fish is subject to availability. Payment can be done at the time of delivery by cash. Orders placed during Hartal/Bandh day will be delivered on the next working day .

Fresh vegetables are procured daily based on the orders. Vegetables are sourced from the best farms in and outside the state. Our vendors are best in the state, doing the business for many decades. We have stocked FMCG and other consumer goods from all renowned brands. We stock goods based on market analysis and consumer response so we are able to provide you with best possible discounts.
